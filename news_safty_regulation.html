Safe and Comfortable

Great reason to choose train travel is that the majority of train carriers have implemented additional regulations to protect travelers' health and safety. If you plan to travel onboard a train, we advise you to familiarize yourself with them before boarding the train to make the traveling experience smoother. You should also consider booking your trip first-class to reduce human contact, protect yourself as well as others and travel comfortably!
Common safety regulations for train travel:

  • Book your tickets in advance to secure your preferred seat and avoid last-minute rush.
  • Research the train route and schedule ahead of time to plan your trip accordingly.
  • Pack light and bring only essential items to avoid hassle and inconvenience.
  • Arrive at the station early to allow time for security checks and boarding.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and footwear for the journey.
  • Keep your valuables, such as passports and money, in a secure place like a money belt or a hidden compartment.
  • Stay hydrated and bring snacks or food for the journey, especially if it's a long one.
  • Follow safety protocols and regulations, such as wearing a mask and practicing social distancing.
  • If you're traveling overnight, consider booking a sleeper or recliner coach for added comfort.
  • Stay alert and aware of your surroundings at all times, and report any suspicious behavior or activity to the authorities immediately.
