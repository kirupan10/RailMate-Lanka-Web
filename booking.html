Sri Lanka Railways, the primary railway operator in Sri Lanka, provides seat reservations for its long-distance trains. With the option to reserve seats online through the Sri Lanka Railways website, passengers can secure a comfortable and hassle-free journey. Sri Lanka Railways offers seat reservations for different classes of travel, including first class, second class, and third class. By reserving a seat in advance, passengers can avoid standing in crowded carriages and enjoy the scenic views from a reserved window seat.
