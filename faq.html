Train Reservation System - Frequently Asked Questions

  • To make a reservation, go to the homepage and enter your travel details, including your departure and arrival stations, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Then, select your preferred train and seat class, and enter your personal details to complete the reservation.

  • Yes, you can change your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled departure time. To change your reservation, go to the "My Reservations" page and select the reservation you wish to modify. Then, follow the prompts to make your changes.

  • Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of ticket you purchased. Please refer to the terms and conditions of your ticket for more information.

  • We accept all major credit cards, as well as PayPal and bank transfers. Please note that some payment methods may not be available in certain regions.

  • You can contact our customer support team by phone, email, or live chat. Our contact information can be found on the "Contact Us" page.
